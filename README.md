# Sales_Dashboard_Power_BI

This project consists of building an interactive Sales Dashboard using Power BI to analyze business performance and extract actionable insights.

It was initially developed as part of my learning journey and then extended with my own analysis and interpretation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track key sales performance metrics  
- Identify trends and seasonality  
- Compare performance across categories and regions  
- Support data-driven decision making  

---

## 📊 Key Features
- Interactive dashboard with slicers and filters  
- KPI tracking (Revenue, Sales Trends, Category Performance)  
- Visual analysis by region and time  
- Clear and structured data visualization  

---

## 🛠️ Data Preparation
- Cleaned and structured raw sales data  
- Handled missing or inconsistent values  
- Prepared dataset for efficient visualization in Power BI  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- The Laptop category generates over 50% of total revenue, indicating a strong dependency on a single category, which may represent a potential business risk.  
- Sales exhibit clear seasonal patterns across months, suggesting alignment with demand cycles or promotional periods.  
- Some regions show high sales volume but lower revenue contribution, which may indicate lower pricing or less profitable product mix.  

---

## ❓ Business Questions Answered
- Which product categories generate the most revenue?  
- What are the monthly sales trends?  
- Which regions are underperforming?
